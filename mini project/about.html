What is Station 01?

Station 01 is a 24/7 radio station that plays a mix of all music genres with the goal of giving the audience an entertaining music and other radio segment. Broadcasting from the City of tabuk, Station 01 plays only the best music, ranging from the 1980's to the newest song we've known. You'll hear legends like Queen, Air Supply, Apo Hiking Society and many more!
